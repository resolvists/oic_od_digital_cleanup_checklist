\section*{\centering DIGITAL CLEANUP CHECKLIST}
\vspace*{0.5cm}

In an era where personal and professional activities are deeply integrated with digital systems, maintaining organized, secure, and efficient digital environments has become essential. A structured digital cleanup checklist provides a comprehensive framework for reviewing files, accounts, devices, cloud storage, subscriptions, and privacy settings to ensure optimal performance and data protection. By systematically addressing storage management, cybersecurity practices, data privacy, and ongoing maintenance, this checklist supports improved productivity, reduced digital clutter, minimized security risks, and stronger control over sensitive information.
